Hello Cutea Streat has been an establishment that has offered food and drinks since 2018. In May 2018, they started selling their home and decided to have an establishment in August 2018. Hello Cutea Streat name and theme of the store is inspired and derived from a one-year-old daughter of the store Owners, Mr. and Mrs. Kristoffer Ryan Baguna, who loves hello kitty stuff. When the pandemic started last 2019, Hello Cutea Streat partnered with Grab and Foodpanda to have more sales because people stay inside their homes and can't go out to buy in the store.
